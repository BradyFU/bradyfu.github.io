Biography

I am a Ph.D. Candidate of CRIPAC (led by Prof. Tieniu Tan), National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) under the supervision of Prof. Ran He. Before that, I received my Bachelor degree (Ranking: 1/134, GPA: 3.86/4.0) from Anhui University (AHU) in 2017 under the supervision of Prof. Shuping He. My research focuses on face recognition and image synthesis.
